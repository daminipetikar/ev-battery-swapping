Glossary

Battery Swapping
The process of replacing a depleted battery with a fully charged one in an electric vehicle.
Swapping Station
A facility where EV batteries are exchanged quickly using robotic or manual systems.
Charging Time
The time it takes to recharge an EV battery using a standard or fast charger.
EV (Electric Vehicle)
A vehicle powered fully or partially by electricity from batteries or fuel cells.
